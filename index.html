ABOUT NOVA NEXUS

Nova Nexus Hub is the official technical association of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Kings Engineering College.

The hub bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world engineering challenges through hands-on activities and student-driven initiatives.

Nova Nexus nurtures innovation, leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills to develop industry-ready engineers.

Core ECE Domains

Embedded Systems, VLSI, DSP, Communication, IoT

OUR VISION

Engineering Tomorrow
Through Innovation

To build a future-focused technical ecosystem that transforms students into innovative, ethical, and industry-ready engineers capable of solving real-world challenges.

01

Technical Excellence

Strengthening core ECE domains through structured learning, hands-on workshops, and project-driven initiatives.

02

Hands-On Learning

Encouraging practical exposure via hackathons, techathons, laboratories, and real-world engineering challenges.

03

Collaborative Culture

Building a peer-driven environment that promotes teamwork, leadership, mentoring, and knowledge sharing.

04

Industry Integration

Bridging academia and industry through expert talks, industrial exposure, and skill-aligned training programs.

05

Impact-Oriented Engineers

Developing engineers who are ethically responsible, adaptable, and capable of delivering sustainable solutions.
